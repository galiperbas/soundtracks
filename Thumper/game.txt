Summary: Thumper is a rhythm violence game. It combines classic rhythm-action with breakneck speed and brutal physicality.
Thumper strips rhythm gaming down to its core. Classic rhythm-action is combined with breakneck speed and brutal physicality. With one analog stick and one button, you control a space beetle while hurtling through stunning and treacherous worlds.
There's no blood or gore, but you'll feel the violence.

Genre: Music, Adventure, Indie, Arcade

Theme: Action, Horror, Thriller
