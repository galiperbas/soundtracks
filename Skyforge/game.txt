Summary: In Skyforge, you will walk the path from an immortal warrior to a mighty God, gaining followers as you fight to protect your world.

Your loyal followers will help you increase your power as you rise to become an Elder God. The best of the best will join the Order of Keepers and shape the fate of the world.

Genre: Role-playing (RPG), Hack and slash/Beat 'em up, Adventure

Theme: Action, Fantasy, Science fiction, Open world
