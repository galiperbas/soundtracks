Summary: Sacred 3 features classic arcade Hack n' Slash action centered around drop in, drop out cooperative multiplayer for both offline (2 players) and online (4 players) play. Develop your character's skills and abilities and band together to use powerful co-op abilities and tactics. Master the art of war and fight your way through enemy hordes to become the greatest hero of all.

Immerse yourself in the the rich lore and fantasy of the Sacred universe. Filled with visually stunning levels, brand new as well as familiar environments, the continent of Ancaria has never felt so alive. Explore this world through the heroes and their cultures and fight the rise of evil until the bitter end.

Genre: Role-playing (RPG), Hack and slash/Beat 'em up, Arcade

Theme: Action, Fantasy
