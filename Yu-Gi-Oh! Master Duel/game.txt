Summary: Yu-Gi-Oh! Master Duel lets players old and new enjoy the hugely popular Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG in digital form. Players across the globe can compete in intense collectible card Duels, both thrilling to play and watch. Alongside those iconic cards will be a huge range of other monsters, spells, traps, and more, drawn from Yu-Gi-Oh's two-decade-long legacy.

Genre: Simulator, Strategy, Card & Board Game

Theme: Fantasy, Kids
