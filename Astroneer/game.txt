Summary: Explore and exploit distant worlds in a game of aerospace industry and interplanetary exploration. With your gun you can reshape the landscape around you and gather resources. On your journey you come across special artifacts that will provide you with research points, in order to unlock new technologies. These include rovers and spaceships, in order the expand your reach on the planets in the solar system. The game also has a co-op mode, allowing players to explore the planets with their friends.

Genre: Simulator, Adventure, Indie

Theme: Action, Science fiction, Sandbox, Kids
