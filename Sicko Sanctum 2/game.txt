Summary: Enemies are weapons. Style is power. Your only goal, total violence. SICKO SANCTUM 2 is an innovative score-attack movement-shooter crafted from the design lessons of modern classics such as ULTRAKILL and HYPER DEMON. Featuring a simple player move-set in combination with synergistic enemy design, SICKO SANCTUM 2 represents the cutting-edge of digitized violence.

Genre: Shooter, Indie, Arcade

Theme: Action, Survival
