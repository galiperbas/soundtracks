Summary: Gravity Rush is an action-adventure video game developed by SIE Japan Studio and Project Siren, and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. Gravity Rush Remastered is the PS4 remaster of the original Gravity Rush game which is bundled with all the previously released downloadable content.

Genre: Platform, Role-playing (RPG), Hack and slash/Beat 'em up, Adventure

Theme: Action, Fantasy, Comedy, Open world
