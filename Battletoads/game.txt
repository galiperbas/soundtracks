Summary: An unreleased game for the Battletoads series which never made it past the alpha stage, and it would've used pre-rendered sprites similar to the Donkey Kong Country series. Only 2 stages were close to completion, being a remake of Ragnarok's Cavern and a side scrolling stage similar to Artic Caverns.

Genre: Fighting, Hack and slash/Beat 'em up

Theme: Action, Science fiction, Comedy
