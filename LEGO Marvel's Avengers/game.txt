Summary: Lego Marvel's Avengers is a Lego-themed action-adventure video game developed by Traveller's Tales and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment. It is the spin-off to Lego Marvel Super Heroes and the second instalment of the Lego Marvel franchise. The game is based on the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Genre: Platform, Puzzle, Hack and slash/Beat 'em up, Adventure

Theme: Action, Comedy, Kids, Open world
