Summary: Betrayer is a first person action adventure game that takes you to the New World at the turn of the 17th century. The year is 1604. You sailed from England expecting to join a struggling colony on the coast of Virginia. Instead, you find only ghosts and mysteries. What catastrophe blighted the land and drained it of color and life?

Genre: Shooter, Adventure, Indie

Theme: Action, Fantasy, Horror, Thriller, Historical, Stealth, Open world, Mystery
