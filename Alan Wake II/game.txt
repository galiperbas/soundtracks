Summary: Alan Wake II is a 2023 psychological horror action-adventure game developed by Remedy Entertainment and published by Epic Games Publishing. The game follows writer Alan Wake as he navigates a nightmarish world where the boundaries between reality and fiction blur. Haunted by dark forces and his troubled past, he must uncover hidden truths while confronting supernatural enemies in a grim, atmospheric environment.

Genre: Shooter, Adventure

Theme: Action, Horror, Survival
