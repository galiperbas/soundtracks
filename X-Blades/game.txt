Summary: In X-Blades, the player takes on the role of the enchanting heroine Ayumi, who survives a breathtaking dance of blades through the Hordes of Darkness. The long-haired beautiful anime, with her pistol blades and mind-blowing leaps, whirls and swirls from one fantastic level to the next.

Genre: Shooter, Role-playing (RPG), Hack and slash/Beat 'em up, Adventure

Theme: Action, Fantasy
