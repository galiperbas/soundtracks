Summary: Project Zomboid is the ultimate in zombie survival. Alone or in MP: you loot, build, craft, fight, farm and fish in a struggle to survive. A hardcore RPG skillset, a vast map, a massively customisable sandbox and a cute tutorial raccoon await the unwary. So how will you die?

Genre: Role-playing (RPG), Simulator, Indie

Theme: Horror, Survival, Sandbox, Open world
