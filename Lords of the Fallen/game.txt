Summary: Lords of the Fallen is a challenging fantasy action RPG set in a harsh world where humanity has defeated its God. When his army starts to re-emerge from its demonic realm, led by the formidable Lords, humanity turns to an unlikely defender for help.

Lords of the Fallen offers a deep and satisfying melee combat system with weapons, armour and skills influencing speed, power and agility. Every fight is both a challenge and a meaningful victory. Powerful and spectacular magic complete the arsenal. Embark on an epic journey of combat, exploration and discovery
to become the world’s salvation… or doom.

Developed by CI Games of Warsaw, in collaboration with Deck 13, a developer based in Germany.

Genre: Role-playing (RPG), Hack and slash/Beat 'em up

Theme: Action, Fantasy
