Summary: Biomutant is an open-world, post-apocalyptic Kung-Fu fable RPG, with a unique martial arts styled combat system allowing you to mix melee, shooting and mutant ability action. A plague is ruining the land and the Tree-of-Life is bleeding death from its roots. The Tribes stand divided. Explore a world in turmoil and define its fate, will you be its savior or lead it to an even darker destiny?

Genre: Role-playing (RPG), Adventure

Theme: Action, Open world
