Summary: A retro, pixel art style adventure in New York, inspired by 80’s cop shows. You are Jack Kelly and you’ve been framed into murder. Now as a regular beat cop you have to find out, who did that. Expect thick crime story, a lot of black humour and all the other things you can find on the streets of Brooklyn.

Genre: Point-and-click, Puzzle, Simulator, Adventure, Indie

Theme: Action, Comedy, Drama
