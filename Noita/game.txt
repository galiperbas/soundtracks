Summary: Noita is a magical action roguelite set in a world where every pixel is physically simulated. Fight, explore, melt, burn, freeze and evaporate your way through the procedurally generated world using spells you've created yourself. Explore a variety of environments ranging from coal mines to freezing wastelands while delving deeper in search for unknown mysteries.

Genre: Shooter, Role-playing (RPG), Simulator, Adventure, Indie, Arcade

Theme: Action, Fantasy, Sandbox
