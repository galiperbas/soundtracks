Summary: The PC version of Final Fantasy IX features high-resolution character models, improved FMVs and modern enhancements such as auto-save, fast-forward and battle boosters. It supports both controller and keyboard/mouse input. The Steam release also includes achievements and allows for community-created mods, including the popular Moguri Mod, which restores original-style backgrounds and audio.

Genre: Role-playing (RPG), Turn-based strategy (TBS), Adventure
