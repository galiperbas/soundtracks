Summary: Crawl is a short (max. for 20 minutes) horror game, where you need to crawl through tight cave spaces, and take photos of people that "went missing" there.

While doing that, you need to keep your flashlight powered by hand cranking it, and constantly replace the camera's cartridge for every photo you take. But beware, you're not alone down there.

Genre: Adventure, Indie

Theme: Action, Horror, Survival
