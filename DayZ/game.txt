Summary: DayZ is a hardcore open-world survival game with only one rule: stay alive, no matter what. How long can you survive a post-apocalyptic world? A land overrun with an infected "zombie" population, where you compete with other survivors for limited resources. Will you team up with strangers and stay strong together? Or play as a lone wolf to avoid betrayal? This is DayZ, this is your story.

Genre: Shooter, Role-playing (RPG), Simulator, Tactical, Adventure, Indie

Theme: Action, Horror, Thriller, Survival, Drama, Sandbox, Open world, Warfare
